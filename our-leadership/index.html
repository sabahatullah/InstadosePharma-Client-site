Chief Scientific Officer

Dr. Douglas K. Macfadden
M.D., C.M., FRCPC, PhD
Work experience
Dr. Macfadden received a B.Sc. in biology from Queen’s University in 1972, a Ph.D from McGill University (Cancer Research Unit) in 1977, and an M.D. from McGill University in 1979. He is the current chairman of the AIDS in-patient subcommittee of the Ontario Ministry of Health Tripartite Committee on Life Support Programmes, and chairman of the AIDS Committee of the University of Toronto, Department of Medicine. Dr. Macfadden is the inventor of five patented technologies, including a method of using Peptide T as an anti-inflammatory agent to manage disease inducing chronic inflammation or chronic immune system activation, a method of using Peptide T and related Peptides in the treatment of HTLV-1 Myelopathy and multiple sclerosis.

Gareth Wiggan
Director of Operations,
Democratic Republic of Congo
Work experience
Mr. Wiggan brings over 20 years of management, marketing and sales experience to the Company. Prior to joining Instadose Pharma, he was President of Genua 1944, Inc., a company based in Toronto, Canada. Mr. Wiggan spearheaded Genua’s development of a rapid test for Perimenopause and Menopause that detects elevated levels of Follicle Stimulating Hormone (“FSH”) in urine. He supervised the clinical trials and the United States Food & Drug Administration (“FDA”) application process for the Genua menopause test. FDA approval was granted in January 2001, making this test only the third women’s health medical diagnostic device in the past 70 years to be FDA approved for Over-The-Counter (OTC) sale. Mr. Wiggan, lived in Japan from 2001-2003, working as a representative for technology transfer to assist the companies, Nippon-Kayaku, Meiji Seika Group, and Rotho Pharmaceutical. Mr. Wiggan (2004-2007) worked as a financial advisor for Sheikh Mohammed Bin Tahnoon Al Nahyan with a focus on the utilization of insurance products for the financing of agricultural projects in the emirate of Abu Dhabi. The result was the successful financing of a multi-million dollar dairy farm via the United States Export-Import Bank in Abu Dhabi. Africa (2008 - present) - Mr. Wiggan working with Dr. D.K. MacFadden and Mr. Grant Sanders to successfully secure contracts that are notarized by host country and Global Affairs Canada, for Tanzania (20 million hectares), Kenya (12 million hectares) and DRC (3 million hectares) of arable land and Shimoni Port/Kenya Fishing rights (200NM) for 3o years.

Director, Mergers & Acquisitions
General Counsel
Work experience
